Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is SaleSphere?

With SaleSphere, you can create virtual agents for outbound or inbound calls. You can select human-like voices, write objectives and call instructions, and your AI agent is ready to make live calls.

2. Can the AI voice agent make outbound voice calls?

Yes, the AI voice agent can easily make outbound calls. You can create outbound call campaigns with a CSV or directly import a list from your CRM. The AI agent is 100% autonomous and can perform real-time calls just like a real human.

3. Can the AI agent receive phone calls?

Yes, your AI agent is fully capable of taking customer phone calls and engaging with humans.

4. Can AI agents transfer a call to a live human agent?

Yes, with workflows, your AI agents can easily transfer a live call to a human agent.

5. Can AI agents book demos and make outbound sales calls?

AI agents are highly capable of making prospecting calls and booking demos with available time slots. You can use workflows for booking demos as per your availability and time zones.

6. Do I need to write code?

No, not a single line. You just write the call objective and call instructions, select a voice, and you are ready to launch your campaign.
